<p class="about-lead">
I'm Aaditya Bhave. I'm a first year at Penn State, and I've been taking things
apart and building better ones for as long as I can remember. If something looks
impossible, or just badly done, that's usually the exact thing I want to make.
</p>

<p class="about-label"><i class="fas fa-robot" aria-hidden="true"></i> Where it started</p>

I've been obsessed with robotics since I was about three years old. Blame the
movies I grew up on: Enthiran, Real Steel, Robocop, and a whole lineup of others
where machines think and move and matter. That feeling never wore off, and
honestly everything I build is really just me chasing it.

<p class="about-label"><i class="fas fa-compass" aria-hidden="true"></i> What I'm into</p>

I've lived and studied across the UK, the US, Canada and India, so I've picked up
a little bit of everywhere along the way. When I'm not building hardware I'm
usually developing Roblox games, playing lawn tennis, buried in a book (any
genre, I'm not picky), or working with music going the whole time. Lately that's
a lot of Anirudh and Tame Impala. I like doing things the unconventional way,
because the normal way is rarely the interesting part.

<p class="about-label"><i class="fas fa-brain" aria-hidden="true"></i> How my brain actually works</p>
<div class="about-grid">
